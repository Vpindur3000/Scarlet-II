# Neural-network files

Scarlet II v1.0.0 does not distribute neural-network weights.

Configure compatible local files with:

- `BerserkNNUEFile` for NNUE;
- `LeelaWeightsFile` for an optional policy/value network.

Suggested neutral local paths:

- `assets/networks/nnue.nn`
- `assets/networks/policy_value.pb`

These filenames are examples only. Compatibility does not imply permission to redistribute a particular network. Verify its author, source, license, and redistribution terms separately.
